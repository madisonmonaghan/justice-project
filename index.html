Our Mission

The Justice Project is a youth-led nonprofit organization dedicated to advancing legal literacy, civic engagement, and equitable access to justice. Through education, advocacy, and community-based initiatives, we empower young people to understand their rights and become informed leaders within their communities.

What We Do

Legal Literacy

We provide educational resources and workshops that introduce students to foundational legal concepts, civic responsibility, and individual rights.

Community Advocacy

Our initiatives are designed and led by students, addressing local justice-related issues through research, dialogue, and service.

Service & Outreach

We collaborate with community organizations to support outreach efforts that promote fairness, accessibility, and public awareness.

About the Founder

The Justice Project was founded by Madison Monaghan, a student committed to youth leadership, civic responsibility, and the belief that justice begins with education.
